## 🧠 Key Insights

* Asia has a significantly larger population and internet user base compared to the Rest of the World dataset.
* Despite having fewer countries in the dataset, Asia generated slightly higher average monthly views.
* T-Series dominates the Asian region, while MrBeast leads globally outside Asia.
* Asia represents one of the largest digital content consumption markets in the world.
* The Rest of the World shows strong creator dominance through individual creators like MrBeast, while Asia is heavily influenced by large entertainment networks such as T-Series.
